Madrid

Madrid is the capital and most populous municipality of Spain. It has almost 3.4 million inhabitants and a metropolitan area population of approximately 7 million.
It is the second-largest city in the European Union (EU), and its monocentric metropolitan area is the second-largest in the EU.
The municipality covers 604.3 km2 (233.3 sq mi) geographical area.

The most see atraction of Madrid

Santiago Bernabeu

Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is a retractable roof football stadium in Madrid, Spain.
With a seating capacity of 78,297, the stadium has the second-largest seating capacity for a football stadium in Spain.

Sol

This bustling square located bang in the centre of Madrid
is one of the city’s most famous sites. With its semi-circular shape,
it's a junction for many of the city’s historical and busiest streets such as Calle Mayor,
